# DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

## Senior AI Engineer — Aries Marine Group

**Reporting to:** CEO
**Department:** Technology & Digital Transformation
**Effective:** May 2026

---

### PURPOSE

Drive the adoption, integration, and continuous upgrade of Artificial Intelligence across Aries Marine's enterprise systems — turning AI from a buzzword into measurable business outcomes across 87 group companies in 20 countries.

---

### KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

**1. AI-Powered ERP Development & Upgrades**
- Own the ERPNext-AI integration platform (ERP Aries) end-to-end — from architecture to deployment
- Upgrade and maintain the AI middleware layer that connects LLM services (Azure OpenAI, Google Gemini) to the ERPNext PostgreSQL database (517+ tables across 22 business modules)
- Build and refine AI-driven seed data generation pipelines that auto-populate realistic marine/offshore business data for new company setups — reducing onboarding time for new group entities from weeks to hours
- Implement structured output generation using Vercel AI SDK to guarantee data quality at scale

**2. AI Feature Delivery**
- Design and ship AI-powered features: smart invoice processing, RAG-based knowledge retrieval for marine operations, NDT inspection auto-validation, procurement chat assistants
- Integrate AI agents that can read, reason, and act on ERP data (vendor lookup, invoice matching, compliance checks)
- Build the AI Wiki system — a living knowledge base of 50+ marine industry entities, equipment, and operational concepts that the AI references for accurate, domain-specific responses

**3. Platform & Infrastructure**
- Manage multi-cloud AI provider relationships (Azure AI Services, Google Vertex AI) — optimize cost, latency, and model selection
- Maintain the PostgreSQL + pgvector infrastructure for embeddings-based search across invoices, suppliers, and operational documents
- Ensure all AI outputs pass Zod schema validation before touching the database — zero corrupt data

**4. AI Strategy & Roadmap**
- Evaluate emerging AI models and providers; recommend when to adopt, switch, or wait
- Translate business needs (faster invoicing, compliance automation, crew scheduling) into AI solution designs
- Present quarterly AI progress to leadership in business terms: cost saved, time reduced, errors caught

**5. Cross-Functional Enablement**
- Train operations, finance, and HSE teams on AI-assisted workflows
- Document all AI systems so they can be maintained by the broader IT team — no single point of failure
- Collaborate with Dubai, Mumbai, and Sharjah offices to tailor AI features per regional compliance (India GST, UAE VAT, Saudi ZATCA)

# DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

## Lead AI Engineer — Aries Group of Companies

**Reporting to:** Founder Chairman & CEO, Sir Sohan Roy SK
**Department:** Technology & Digital Transformation
**Effective:** May 2026

---

### PURPOSE

Bring artificial intelligence out of the lab and into the daily operations of Aries Group — making every one of our 87 companies across 20 countries faster, more consistent, and more profitable. This role exists to ensure AI serves the Aries way: *"Time is Money… We Count Even Seconds."*

---

### KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

**1. AI-Powered ERP Systems — Making New Companies Operational in Hours, Not Weeks**

When Aries acquires or launches a new company, the ERP system must be ready on day one — complete with realistic business data, financial structures, and operational workflows tailored to that company's country and industry.

- Build and maintain the AI engine that auto-generates complete business setup data for any new Aries entity — companies, bank accounts, tax structures, suppliers, warehouses, equipment — all populated automatically by AI in under 2 hours
- Ensure every piece of data the AI creates is validated before it enters the system — zero errors, zero corruption, zero manual cleanup
- Currently: this onboarding process takes 2–3 weeks of manual data entry per company. Target: under 2 hours per company
- With 87 companies and growing, this alone saves thousands of hours annually

**2. AI Features That Save Time and Catch Errors**

- Smart invoice processing: AI reads incoming invoices, matches them to purchase orders and suppliers, flags discrepancies before they become payment errors
- Intelligent knowledge retrieval: When a project manager in Dubai needs to know which suppliers are qualified for subsea welding, or a safety officer in Mumbai needs NDT inspection standards, the AI answers instantly — pulling from Aries' own operational knowledge base built over 25+ years and 74,607+ projects
- Automated compliance: AI ensures India GST, UAE VAT, and regional tax rules are applied correctly on every transaction — no manual tax lookups, no filing errors
- Procurement assistant: AI suggests the right supplier, the right price, and flags when a better alternative exists — cutting procurement cycle time by 50%+

**3. AI Strategy & Cost Management**

- Continuously evaluate which AI models and providers give Aries the best results at the lowest cost — and switch when better options emerge
- Track every dollar spent on AI infrastructure and report savings vs. manual processes quarterly
- Ensure AI capabilities scale as Aries grows — from 87 companies today to 100+ without system redesign
- All AI systems must be documented and transferable — no single point of failure, no knowledge locked in one person's head

**4. Cross-Company Efficiency Alignment with EFFISM**

- Align AI tool development with EFFISM principles — every AI feature should enhance individual efficiency and transform users into Hartoise professionals (fast like a hare, consistent like a tortoise)
- Design AI workflows that support the 1:1:1 principle (Mind, Body, Karma) — automating repetitive karma so professionals focus on mind and body development
- Enable the 1,000+ Aries employees across 78 subdivisions to use AI as a daily efficiency multiplier, not just an IT tool
- Support EFFISM's 100 efficiency parameters by providing AI-powered measurement, tracking, and improvement recommendations

**5. Regional & Cross-Functional Delivery**

- Tailor AI features per region: India (GST compliance, Mumbai operations), UAE (VAT, Dubai/Sharjah offices), Saudi Arabia (ZATCA), and other operating countries
- Train operations, finance, HSE, and project teams in Dubai, Mumbai, Sharjah, Abu Dhabi, and Fujairah on AI-assisted workflows
- Present AI progress to leadership in business language: time saved, errors caught, cost reduced, efficiency improved

**6. Weekly AI Updates — Keeping Every Employee Informed and Ahead**

No AI tool is useful if people don't know it exists or how to use it. This responsibility ensures every person in Aries stays current with what AI can do for them — every single week.

- Ensure every employee across all 78 subdivisions has access to the latest AI capabilities relevant to their role — no one discovers a time-saving feature months after it launched
- Curate department-specific weekly digests: finance gets AI updates on invoice automation, operations gets procurement and scheduling insights, HSE gets compliance and inspection AI news, project teams get timeline and resource AI tips — each department sees what matters to them
- Track adoption and engagement weekly: who is using what, which features are underutilized, where training is needed — and act on it before gaps widen
