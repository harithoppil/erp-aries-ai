### KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

**1. AI-Powered ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) Systems — Modernising Our Existing Platform**

Aries Group runs on an ERP system that manages everything from invoices and supplier payments to warehouse stock, project timelines, and compliance filings across 87 companies. This system works — but it was built for manual data entry and yesterday's pace. The responsibility is to modernise it with AI: make it faster, smarter, and self-populating, while keeping it rock-solid reliable.



When a new employee joins Aries , the ERP system must be ready and learnable on day one — complete with realistic business data, financial structures, and operational workflows tailored to that individuals's role and dept.



- Modernise the existing ERP platform by embedding AI into its core workflows — from data entry and invoice matching to compliance checks and supplier selection — turning manual processes into intelligent, automated ones
- Build and maintain the AI engine that auto-generates complete business setup data for any new Aries entity — companies, bank accounts, tax structures, suppliers, warehouses, equipment — all populated automatically by AI in under 2 hours
- Automate all manual data entry in all departments.
- With 87 companies and growing, this alone saves thousands of hours annually.
